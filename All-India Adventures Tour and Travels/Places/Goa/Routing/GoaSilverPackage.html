Details Of The SILVER Package




    Day 1: Arrival in Goa


    🌟 Welcome to the vibrant state of Goa! Upon arrival at Goa International Airport, you'll be greeted by our representative who will escort you to your comfortable 3-star hotel in North Goa. Check-in and relax after your journey. Enjoy the evening at leisure, exploring the nearby area or unwinding at the hotel.


    Day 2: Coastal Delights


    🏖️ After a delicious breakfast at the hotel, embark on a sightseeing tour to some of North Goa's most iconic destinations. Visit Calangute Beach, known for its golden sands and vibrant atmosphere. Continue to Fort Aguada, offering panoramic views of the Arabian Sea.

    🕌 Next, explore the Basilica of Bom Jesus and Se Cathedral, two UNESCO World Heritage Sites showcasing exquisite Portuguese architecture. Visit the Church of St. Cajetan before heading to the viewpoint at Dudhsagar Waterfalls for breathtaking vistas.

    🌅 In the evening, experience the magic of a sunset cruise on the Mandovi River. Relax on board as you watch the sun dip below the horizon, casting a golden glow over the water.


    Day 3: Departure


    ☀️ After breakfast, check out from the hotel and bid farewell to Goa. Our representative will assist you with your transfer to Goa International Airport for your onward journey.


    Experience the beauty and charm of Goa in just 3 days, filled with memorable sightseeing tours and delightful experiences.
