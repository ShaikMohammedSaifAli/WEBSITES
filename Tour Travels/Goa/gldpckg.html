Places To Visit In This Package

    Calangute Beach

    Calangute Beach Known as the "Queen of Beaches," Calangute is one of the most popular and largest beaches in North Goa. It's famous for its golden sands, water sports activities, shacks serving delicious seafood, and vibrant nightlife.

      Activities To Be

      Parasailing offers an exhilarating experience as participants soar above the sea. With a parachute-like canopy, they enjoy breathtaking views of the beach and surroundings. Safety is paramount, with operators supplying harnesses and life jackets. Sessions typically last 10-15 minutes, providing ample time to savor the adventure. Prices vary based on factors like duration and season, ranging from ₹800 to ₹1500 per person.

      Banana Boat Rides offer group excitement as participants ride an inflatable banana-shaped boat towed by a speedboat. Riders enjoy bouncing and gliding over waves for a thrilling adventure. Safety is ensured with provided life jackets and thorough operator safety measures. Rides typically last 15-20 minutes, depending on the chosen package. Prices range from ₹300 to ₹500 per person, making it an affordable and fun water activity.

      Jet Skiing provides a high-speed water adventure, with participants riding small watercraft propelled by a jet of water. Riders can zoom through waves. Safety is paramount, with provided life jackets and thorough safety briefings before the activity. Rental durations typically range from 15 minutes to 1 hour, depending on the chosen package. Costs vary based on rental duration and operator, averaging around ₹500 to ₹1000 for a 15-minute ride.

    Fort Aguada

    Fort Aguada, a 17th-century Portuguese fortress in Goa, boasts robust walls, bastions, and a lighthouse, offering panoramic views of the Arabian Sea. Guided tours provide insights into its maritime history, while preservation efforts maintain its architectural integrity, making it a symbol of Goa's colonial heritage.

      Activities To Be

      Historical exploration at Fort Aguada unveils its significance as a 17th-century Portuguese fortress guarding against invaders like the Dutch and Marathas. Its robust architecture, including walls, bastions, and a historic lighthouse, reflects its strategic position overlooking the Arabian Sea. Visitors can delve into its military past, exploring gun emplacements, barracks, and storage areas while enjoying panoramic views of the coastline. Guided tours and interpretive signage offer insights into its construction, maritime history, and preservation efforts. Fort Aguada stands as a captivating testament to Goa's colonial heritage, inviting visitors to immerse themselves in its rich historical narrative.

      Visiting the lighthouse at Fort Aguada offers panoramic views of the Arabian Sea and the coastline, providing a unique perspective on maritime history. Ascend to the top for breathtaking vistas, typically accessed via a spiral staircase or steps. Guided tours provide insights into the lighthouse's history, operation, and significance in navigation. The lighthouse's architectural design reflects Portuguese colonial heritage, making it an architectural marvel worth exploring. Preservation efforts ensure that this historic landmark remains accessible for visitors to appreciate its beauty and historical importance.

      Relaxing on Sinquerim Beach adjacent to Fort Aguada offers a serene escape with its soft golden sands and clear turquoise waters. Visitors can sunbathe, swim in the calm Arabian Sea, or partake in thrilling water sports like parasailing and jet-skiing. Sinquerim Beach is family-friendly, equipped with amenities like restrooms and showers, ensuring a comfortable and enjoyable beach experience for all.

    Se Cathedral

    The Se Cathedral in Old Goa is a magnificent example of Portuguese-Gothic architecture, dating back to the 16th century. Dedicated to St. Catherine of Alexandria, it is one of the largest churches in Asia and houses the mortal remains of St. Francis Xavier. Its grandeur, intricate detailing, and religious significance make it a must-visit landmark for tourists and pilgrims alike.


      Activities To Be

      Exploring the architecture of Se Cathedral unveils majestic facades adorned with intricate carvings depicting the life of St. Catherine of Alexandria. Step inside to encounter soaring arches, ornate altars, and a spacious nave, exuding grandeur and reverence. Admire the vaulted ceilings adorned with exquisite frescoes, while stained glass windows filter sunlight into a colorful spectrum. Discover side chapels and altars embellished with fine artistry, reflecting the cathedral's rich spiritual heritage. Pay attention to intricate architectural details, from delicate tracery to finely carved stone, adding to the cathedral's beauty and significance.

      Visiting the Chapel of St. Francis Xavier in Se Cathedral allows a reverent encounter with the saint's mortal remains housed in a silver casket. Learn about his missionary endeavors and religious significance through informative displays. Experience a moment of spiritual reflection and prayer in the chapel's tranquil ambiance. Admire the architectural beauty of the chapel, reflecting reverence and devotion. Join pilgrims from around the world in seeking solace and blessings at this revered pilgrimage destination.

      Attending Mass in Se Cathedral offers a serene and spiritual experience, immersed in prayers and hymns. Join fellow worshippers in the celebration of the Eucharist, fostering a sense of unity and devotion. Receive the sacraments and encounter the grace and blessings of the Mass. Reflect on the Gospel teachings and find inspiration for your spiritual journey. Connect with the local faith community, experiencing the universal fellowship of the Catholic Church.

    Dudhsagar Waterfalls

    Dudhsagar Waterfalls in Goa, India, boasts a towering height of around 310 meters, earning its name from the frothy appearance of its cascading waters. A visit to the viewpoint offers a mesmerizing panoramic view amidst lush greenery, providing an unforgettable encounter with the raw power and beauty of nature.

      Activities To Be

      Dudhsagar Waterfalls offers exciting trekking opportunities for adventurers. Trails wind through dense forests, providing glimpses of diverse flora and fauna. Trekking to the base of the falls is particularly popular, offering close-up views of the cascading waters and an exhilarating experience. The trek is moderately challenging, with uneven terrain and occasional steep climbs, making it suitable for adventure enthusiasts of various skill levels.

      The surrounding forests of Dudhsagar are rich in biodiversity, making it an ideal spot for wildlife spotting. Trekking trails offer chances to encounter various species of birds, butterflies, and small mammals amidst the verdant foliage. Lucky visitors may catch sight of langurs, bison, deer, and even leopards in their natural habitat. Birdwatchers can also enjoy spotting endemic and migratory bird species, adding an exciting dimension to the nature experience.

      Dudhsagar Waterfalls provides a picturesque setting for a leisurely picnic amidst nature's tranquility. Visitors can relax by the banks of the river or find shady spots under towering trees. The soothing sound of the cascading waters creates a serene ambiance, perfect for unwinding with loved ones. Packing a picnic basket with delicious snacks and refreshments adds to the enjoyment, allowing visitors to savor the scenic beauty while indulging in a delightful outdoor meal.

    Dona Paula

    Perched atop a cliff overlooking the Arabian Sea in Goa, India, Dona Paula viewpoint offers breathtaking panoramic vistas. Renowned for its romantic allure and stunning sunsets, this scenic spot attracts visitors seeking tranquility and natural beauty. From here, one can admire the confluence of the Mandovi and Zuari rivers as they merge into the vast expanse of the ocean.

      Activities To Be

      Dona Paula viewpoint offers photographers a plethora of opportunities to capture stunning landscapes and seascapes. From the vantage point atop the cliff, visitors can photograph the azure waters of the Arabian Sea stretching to the horizon, the picturesque coastline, and the verdant surroundings. The soft hues of the sky during sunrise and sunset create captivating backdrops for memorable shots. Additionally, the iconic view of the confluence of the Mandovi and Zuari rivers adds a unique element to photographic compositions, making it a favorite spot for photography enthusiasts.

      While Dona Paula viewpoint itself may not directly offer water sports activities, nearby beaches such as Dona Paula Beach and Miramar Beach provide ample opportunities for aquatic adventures. Visitors can indulge in thrilling activities like jet skiing, parasailing, banana boat rides, and windsurfing, among others. The calm waters of these beaches coupled with the gentle sea breeze create ideal conditions for water sports enthusiasts of all levels to enjoy exhilarating experiences and make unforgettable memories.

      Apart from its scenic beauty and water sports opportunities, Dona Paula serves as a tranquil retreat for relaxation and leisure. Visitors can unwind on the sandy shores of Dona Paula Beach or Miramar Beach, soaking up the sun and listening to the soothing sound of waves crashing against the shore. The well-maintained promenade at Dona Paula offers a perfect setting for leisurely strolls, allowing visitors to immerse themselves in the coastal ambiance while enjoying panoramic views of the sea. Additionally, the area surrounding Dona Paula is dotted with quaint cafes and eateries where visitors can savor delicious coastal cuisine and refreshments, further enhancing the leisurely experience.

    Baga Beach

    Baga Beach, nestled along the coastline of North Goa, India, is renowned for its vibrant atmosphere and picturesque setting. With its golden sands stretching for miles, it offers the perfect backdrop for sunbathing, beach games, and leisurely walks. Visitors can also enjoy a variety of water sports activities, beachside dining, and lively nightlife, making Baga Beach a popular destination for tourists seeking sun, sea, and excitement.

      Activities To Be

      The vast expanse of golden sands at Baga Beach invites visitors to relax and soak up the sun. Sunbathers can spread out their towels, lounge chairs, or beach mats and bask in the warmth while listening to the rhythmic sounds of the waves. For those seeking a bit of activity, the beach is perfect for playing beach volleyball, football, or frisbee with friends and family. It's a fantastic way to bond with loved ones while enjoying the stunning coastal scenery.

      As the sun sets, Baga Beach comes alive with pulsating energy and vibrant nightlife. Beachside bars and clubs host lively parties with DJs spinning tunes, live music performances, and fire shows. Join fellow revelers for a night of dancing under the stars or relax with a drink in hand while enjoying the electrifying ambiance of Baga's nightlife scene.

      Embark on an unforgettable boat trip from Baga Beach to explore the coastline and nearby attractions. Many operators offer dolphin-watching excursions where you can spot playful dolphins frolicking in the waves. It's a magical experience that adds an extra dimension to your visit to Baga Beach.
